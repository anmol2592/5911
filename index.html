Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu (11 June 1993 - 29 May 2022), better known by his stage name Sidhu Moose Wala, was an Indian rapper. He rose to the mainstream with his track "So High". In 2018, he released his debut album , which peaked at number 66 on the Billboard Canadian Albums chart.
